Welcome to the CacheLib blog! This is where we will share feature announcements, deprecation notices, and any important changes that we want our users to be aware of.

<!-- truncate -->

## What to Expect

- **Feature announcements** — Learn about new capabilities as they land in CacheLib.
- **Deprecation notices** — Stay informed about APIs or features being phased out, along with migration guidance.
- **Design discussions** — Deep dives into the thinking behind CacheLib's architecture and design decisions.
- **Reflections** — Lessons learned, performance insights, and observations from running CacheLib at scale.

Stay tuned for more. In the meantime, check out our [User Guide](/docs/) and [Architecture Guide](/docs/Cache_Library_Architecture_Guide/overview_a_random_walk) to get started with CacheLib.
